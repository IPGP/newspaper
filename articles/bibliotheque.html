Bibliothèque nationale de France
From Wikipedia

The Bibliothèque nationale de France (BnF; French: [bi.bli.jɔ.tɛk na.sjɔ.nal də fʁɑ̃s]) is the National Library of France, located in Paris. It is the national repository of all that is published in France. The current president of the library is Laurence Engel.
